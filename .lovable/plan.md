|-------|-----------|

| PRIORITIZE | Issue detection and meeting integration |
| ACT | Interventions (intentional improvement changes) |

## Section 5: FAQ Section
**File:** `src/pages/Index.tsx` (inline section)

### Rewritten FAQs

| # | Current Question | Rewritten Question |
|---|------------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Do I need to use EOS to benefit from ClinicLeader? | Do I need to follow a specific framework to use ClinicLeader? |
| 2 | How long does it take to get started? | How long does it take to get started? |
| 3 | Will this create more work for my team? | Will this create more work for my team? |
| 4 | Is my data secure? | Is my data secure? |
| 5 | Can I import existing metrics and goals? | What data sources can ClinicLeader connect to? |

**Rewritten Answers:**

1. **Do I need to follow a specific framework to use ClinicLeader?**
   "No. ClinicLeader provides structure inspired by proven operating systems, but you don't need formal training or certifications. The system guides you through scorecards, meetings, and improvement tracking."

2. **How long does it take to get started?**
   "Most clinics run their first structured weekly meeting within two weeks. Setup includes connecting your data source, importing existing goals, and inviting your leadership team."

3. **Will this create more work for my team?**
   "The opposite. ClinicLeader replaces scattered spreadsheets, fragmented meeting prep, and untracked improvement efforts. Teams spend less time gathering information and more time making decisions."

4. **Is my data secure?**
   "Yes. Your clinic data remains yours. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow healthcare data security best practices. Cross-clinic learning uses only anonymized, aggregated patterns - never identifiable information."

5. **What data sources can ClinicLeader connect to?**
   "ClinicLeader connects to Jane App, other EMR systems, Excel or spreadsheet uploads, and supports manual entry for operational metrics. No disruption to your current workflows is required."
